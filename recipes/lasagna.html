Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ricotta cheese
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste

How To:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Drain and transfer to a bowl.
